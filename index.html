About

Topup - Mart

Our Services provides recharge service for all major prepaid mobile service providers in srilanka. We aim at empowering our users by providing quick and convenient prepaid mobile recharge as well as bill payments. Our Services is completely reliable in terms of services and security. We at Our Services believe that customer satisfaction should be the primary objective of any successful company.

Services We Provide

Admin

Our admin software enable you to offer a wide range of multi recharge services, including prepaid and postpaid mobile recharge, Utility Bill and more. Create Unlimited Retailer panel , Create Unlimited distributor panel , Create Unlimited M distributor panel , Create Unlimited API user.

Branding

Get a branded website and android app with user-friendly control panel to start your own recharge company. Your name website , Your company logo , Android application , Your contact details , Your company branding.

API Partners

Choose Topup - Mart as your recharge API provider and enjoy the benefits of our real-time recharge facility with an instant success response.Expand your business opportunities by becoming a trusted recharge Api partners.

Master Distibuter

As a multi recharge Master distributor, you gain access to add unlimited retailer and offer wide range of recharge services, including mobile recharges, postpaid bill, utility bill payments, Insurance and loan payments more.

Distibuter

As a multi recharge distributor, you gain access to add unlimited retailer and offer wide range of recharge services, including mobile recharges, postpaid bill, utility bill payments, Insurance and loan payments more.

Retailer

Expand your business opportunities by becoming a trusted recharge retailer. Increase revenue and customer base. Seamless recharge, competitive commission, and reliable support. Start your journey today!
